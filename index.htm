Dragon Ball Farmz is a farming simulator and idle game on the blockchain. The more training you do, the more ki you gain (each hour of training generates 1 ki per day). Focus your ki to train more and multiply your production, or disperse it for BNB!

Powered by Metamask

This game requires Metamask

Automated Market

Dragon Ball Farmz features a high tech automated market that lets you instantly focus or disperse ki to gain BNB. Driven by supply and demand, the price automatically adjusts as players trade.
